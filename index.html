How To Play

You must draw a line that traverses the tiles in a way that turns them all blue.

White tiles must be traversed once; yellow tiles twice; pink tiles three times; purple tiles four times.

You may not move your line in the same direction as you did on your last move.

Your line may not be drawn across another line, though they may intersect.
